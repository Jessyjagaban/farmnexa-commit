How FarmNexa Works

Simple, transparent, and direct connection between farmers and consumers

For Farmers

  1. Register & Setup Profile

    Create your account and add farm details

  2. List Your Produce

    Uploads photos, set prices, and manage inventory

  3. Receive Orders

    Get instant notification when customers order

  4. Deliver & Earn

    Fulfill orders and receive direct payments

For Consumers

  1. Browse Fresh Produce

    Discover local fresh produce from nearby farms

  2. Add to Cart

    Select items and quantites you need

  3. Choose Payment

    Pay via bank transfer or cash on delivery

  4. Receive Fresh Produce

    Get your order delivered or pick up locally
